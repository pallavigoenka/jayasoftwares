EEPC India

In 2005, Jaya Softwares took up the task of revamping the EEPC India website and re-launched it under the present URL – www.eepcindia.org. During the course of development, a number of modules and functions were added to what was initially launched. It is one of the most comprehensive and updated web portals among its peers in India including other EPCs, Chambers of Commerce, Trade Bodies, etc. Over the years, some of our successfully implemented online projects for the Engineering Exports Promotion Council (EEPC) include:

  • An Online Application System for EEPC National Awards
  • An Online Survey System for Member Feedback
  • A Delegate Management System implemented in all IESS shows
  • An Exhibitor Management System implemented in IESS IV
  • An Online Change Request System for Information Update by members

TDSMAN And ChequeMan

TDSMAN & ChequeMan are two software products developed by PDS Infotech (P) Ltd. TDSMAN was launched in 2011 and ChequeMan in 2012. Both these products have the following dedicated websites that have been developed by our team at Jaya Softwares:

  • www.tdsman.com
  • www.chequeman.com

Both the products, in a very short span of time are the market leaders in their own segment having a combined base of 30,000 users across India. Surely the products have their functional and usability merits which are the cornerstones of their success. Our contribution to this success story has been the online presence of both these products. Almost 75% of the business for both the products is transacted online.

Our development team has been handling the following online/ web-based activities:

  • Defining and continuously evaluating/ reviewing the web strategy
  • Development & maintenance of the websites ever since product launch
  • Development of mobile friendly websites for both products in 2014
  • Online management of the system for download & activation of Trial Software. An automated internal system for lead nurturing using system based email for reminders and follow-ups
  • Development, implementation & support to the web-based registration of licensed software with a robust model for online delivery of product updates
  • Development & implementation & support to the web-based Helpdesk for rendering efficient telephonic / online support to the product users.
  • Many other micro web-based protected systems for efficient product and service management

Our digital marketing team has been handling the following activities: 

  • Managing AdWord campaigns in Google, Bing & Yahoo
  • Managing TDSMAN Blog – on an average there are around 10,000 visitors each day
  • On-Page and Off-Page SEO activities
  • Email Marketing – including designing of creative
  • Social Media Presence – Facebook, LinkedIn & Twitter
  • MobileApp for TDSMAN Blog

On an average around 200 business leads are generated on a daily basis through the digital marketing effort by our team.

Expo Display Service, Dubai

Expo Display Service is one the leading companies in UAE providing modular and customized exhibition stand building solutions for exhibition and display purposes. Jaya Softwares has developed their interactive web platform (www.expodisplayservice.ae) wherein one can visually browse through the various solutions and products offered by them. The portfolio of their past projects is also showcased on the website.

The strength of the website is in its navigation simplicity and helps potential customers identify a suitable solution with minimum effort. With strategic effort from us in digital marketing, EDS has been able to generate a good amount of online leads both through organic search and paid campaigns. 

Its success has prompted its stakeholders, to use our services for their other global companies located in USA (www.exponents.com) & India (www.insta-group.com). These websites have been launched very recently (December 2014).

EICMA

In 2007, we developed the website of the Eastern Region Corrugated Box Manufacturers’ Association (www.eicbma.com) – which included the unique online ‘Compliance Calendar’. The content and the platform for routine updates made it one of the most popular destinations for industry related information in India.

EICMA organized the national conference for the industry in 2009 for which Jaya Softwares did the conference website (http://www.eicbma.com/38fcbm/) including delegate & exhibitor data management. This website became the benchmark for all annual conferences organized by different regions as per their turn. We were invited to develop the conference websites for other associations of the industry such as the North India Corrugated Box Manufacturers Association (December 2011) and the Western India Corrugated Box Manufacturers Association (December 2012).

Continuing the success of these projects – we were approached by the Indian Corrugated Case Manufacturers’ Association (ICCMA) for providing web solutions for their various activities. We initially developed the ICCMA website (www.iccma.com) and an online platform for member interaction (www.iccma.com/forum). This was followed by the development of websites for the ICCMA Congress (Conference and Exhibition) in the years 2013 and 2014.
